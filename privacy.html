Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 06/18/2026]

This Privacy Policy describes how Brian Madl's Personal SMS Assistant handles information in connection with its SMS messaging service.

Service Description

Brian Madl's Personal SMS Assistant is a personal-use SMS service operated by Brian Madl The service is intended only for use by the operator to send SMS messages to the operator’s own phone number and to receive messages from that same authorized phone number.

This service is not used for marketing, advertising, lead generation, public messaging campaigns, or communication with customers or third-party users.

Information Collected

When SMS messages are sent to or from this service, the following information may be collected or processed:

  • Phone numbers involved in the SMS exchange
  • SMS message contents
  • Message timestamps
  • Delivery status and related metadata
  • Technical logs needed to operate, troubleshoot, and secure the service

How Information Is Used

Information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Sending SMS messages to the authorized personal phone number
  • Receiving SMS messages from the authorized personal phone number
  • Operating and maintaining the SMS service
  • Troubleshooting delivery or technical issues
  • Preventing unauthorized or abusive use
  • Maintaining security and reliability of the service

The service does not use SMS data for advertising, marketing, resale, profiling, or lead generation.

SMS Consent and Mobile Information

Mobile information, including phone numbers and SMS opt-in consent, will not be sold, rented, shared, or transferred to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

All categories above exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent. This information will not be shared with any third parties, except as necessary to provide and operate the messaging service.

Third-Party Service Providers

This service uses Twilio to send and receive SMS messages. Twilio may process phone numbers, message contents, delivery metadata, and related information as necessary to provide SMS messaging services.

Twilio’s handling of information is subject to Twilio’s own policies and terms.

No Marketing Use

This SMS service does not send promotional or marketing messages. It is used only for personal assistant messages, testing, alerts, notifications, and automation messages sent to the operator’s own authorized phone number.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You may reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

Data Retention

SMS message logs and related metadata may be retained as needed for service operation, troubleshooting, security, and audit purposes. Data may be deleted periodically when it is no longer needed.

Access Restrictions

This service is intended only for the operator’s personal use. Unauthorized use of this SMS service is not permitted.
